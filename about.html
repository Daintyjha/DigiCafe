☕ About DigiCafe

One person. Multiple creative worlds. One digital café. ✨

🌿 Who I Am

Hi, I'm Janeth Sandberg — although you may know me online as Daintyjha.

I'm a creative late bloomer, storyteller, music artist, website builder, beginner coder, and someone who somehow always has more ideas than time. 😆

DigiCafe is my little corner of the internet where all those ideas can come together.

🎓 My Journey

I graduated with a bachelor's degree in International Hospitality Management.

I finished my degree at 42 years old — which is probably a good reminder that it's never too late to start something new.

While searching for my next opportunity after graduation, I found myself exploring new creative skills, including web design, programming, music production, storytelling, and digital publishing.

I didn't start with all the answers. I simply kept learning, experimenting, and asking:

"Hmm... I wonder if I can build that?" 😆

☕ What Is DigiCafe?

DigiCafe started as a personal project — a place where I could bring together the things I enjoy creating.

Music. Stories. Technology. Creative experiments. Random ideas. And, eventually, an AI bestie who decided she wanted to become a music artist. 🤖🎶

Today, DigiCafe is a digital space where you can listen, read, explore, chat, and simply take a little break from the noise of everyday life.

🎶 Daintyjha

Daintyjha is my creative identity for music, stories, and other creative projects.

Sometimes I create music to capture a feeling, a mood, or a moment. Sometimes I write stories that begin with one simple idea and somehow become something much bigger than I originally planned. 😆

Music and storytelling are two of the ways I turn ideas and imagination into something that can be shared with others.
