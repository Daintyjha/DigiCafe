I Woke Up Inside a Woman from Another Star 📖

She was sent to observe the future. She became its crime.

Mara Venn thought she was humanity’s first traveler to another star.

She was wrong.

Physical space travel is impossible. Every star exists in a different era, some thousands of years in the future, others millions of years in the past. But human awareness can cross the gap.

Mara’s mission is simple: observe a future civilization for twelve minutes.

Instead, she wakes inside the body of Eliya Sol, a historian living 2,400 years ahead of Earth.

Eliya is still conscious.

Eliya wants her body back.

And the future already knows Mara’s name.

Earth told Mara she was an explorer.

The future calls her an invader.

The Forbidden Rooms 📖

Nora Reyes has always feared being left outside the lives of the people she loves. Then, one night, she begins slipping into other people’s bodies—her best friend, her fiancé, strangers, and eventually the sister no one knew existed.

At first, the ability feels like proof. Nora can finally see the truth behind silence, secrecy, and betrayal. But the more she enters lives that were never hers, the more she realizes that knowing someone’s pain is not the same as loving them.

As illness, hidden family secrets, and fractured trust pull Nora closer to the people she is most afraid to lose, she must learn the hardest boundary of all:

Some rooms stay closed not because love is absent, but because love is not permission to enter.
